FAQ

Q1: How do solar panels work?▼

A1: Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity through photovoltaic cells. These cells contain a semiconductor material, often silicon, which absorbs photons of light and releases electrons, generating an electric current.

Q2: What are the benefits of installing solar panels at home?▼

A2: - Cost Savings: Solar panels can significantly reduce or even eliminate your electricity bill.
- Environmental Impact: They reduce your carbon footprint by using renewable energy.
- Energy Independence: Less reliance on grid electricity, which can be beneficial during outages.
- Increased Property Value: Homes with solar installations often have higher resale values.
