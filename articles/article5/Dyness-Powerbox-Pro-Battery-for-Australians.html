Manufacturer Background: About Dyness

Dyness is a global leader in energy storage solutions, specialising in advanced lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO₄) technology. The Powerbox Pro is designed specifically for residential solar storage in Australia, delivering safe, efficient, and scalable energy management. With a strong emphasis on durability and safety, Dyness products are built to perform reliably in diverse Australian conditions—from hot inland regions to coastal climates.

Dyness Powerbox Pro Technical Specifications

The Powerbox Pro is a low-voltage (51.2 V) DC-coupled battery engineered with LiFePO₄ cells. Its design allows for flexible, modular installation, ideal for both new solar systems and retrofits. Key technical specifications include:

  • Capacity & Scalability: 10.24 kWh per unit (approximately 9.2 kWh usable at 90% depth of discharge). Units can be paralleled up to 5, providing a total storage capacity of ~51 kWh.
  • Power Output: Continuous output of ~5.12 kW with peak output reaching 10.24 kW, making it suitable for handling household loads and surge requirements.
  • Efficiency: Achieves a round-trip efficiency of ~95% due to minimal DC-to-DC conversion losses.
  • Cycle Life & Warranty: Rated for over 6,000 cycles at 90% DoD, with a 10-year warranty guaranteeing at least 70% capacity retention.
  • Safety & Communication: Integrated Battery Management System (BMS) ensures comprehensive protection (overcharge, over-discharge, over-current, short-circuit) and offers communication via CAN Bus, RS485, or RS232.

Installation & Integration

The Dyness Powerbox Pro is engineered for flexible installation. Its compact design allows for both wall and floor mounting, making it ideal for various spaces—from garages and utility rooms to sheltered outdoor areas. It connects directly to compatible DC-coupled hybrid inverters, integrating seamlessly with solar arrays sized at 5–8 kW for a standard 10 kWh unit. Professional installation by Clean Energy Council accredited installers ensures compliance with Australian standards and optimal system performance.

Certifications & Safety

Safety is paramount with the Powerbox Pro. The unit is CEC-approved for the Australian market and meets several international safety standards, including IEC 62619 for cell safety, IEC 62040 for UPS components, and UN38.3 for safe transportation. These certifications, along with an IP65-rated enclosure, ensure that the battery is both safe and durable in a variety of environmental conditions.
